Nurturing Excellence Since 2006

Located in the heart of Fatehpur Khas, Parsauli (Ambedkar Nagar), Madarsa Faizul Uloom Parsauli has been a beacon of knowledge for nearly two decades.

Our mission is to empower the next generation by providing a balanced education that combines modern academic standards with deep-rooted moral values.

  • UP Madarsa Board Recognized: Following a government-approved curriculum for standardized primary education.
  • Holistic Development: Focusing on Hindi, English, Mathematics, and Computer Literacy alongside Urdu studies.
  • Experienced Faculty: Guided by Head Teacher Virender and a team of 7 dedicated professionals committed to student success.
